In-Line Elements

In-Line Elements expand to the size of the container they are in. Containers of In-Line Elements do not stretch to the whole width of the screen.

In-Line Elements will be positioned side-by-side on the same line

the most common In-Line Elements are the anchor <a > <a∕> tag

and the image <img > tag
